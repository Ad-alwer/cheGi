# Charity Commitment

cheGi pledges **20% of all funding** to charity.

We believe in building tools that help developers do more — and giving back to the people who need it most. Every contribution to cheGi is a contribution to a better world.

**Where the funds go:**

- Local aid and charities supporting those in need
- Disaster relief and humanitarian causes
- Developer communities and open-source initiatives

**Why this matters:**

cheGi was built by developers, for developers. But our impact doesn't have to stop at the terminal. By supporting cheGi, you're not just getting a better Git companion — you're helping people who need it most.

**The future:**

We have big plans. As cheGi grows, we'll do more — for our users and for the world. Help cheGi grow, and everyone benefits.
